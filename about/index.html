A LITTLE ABOUT ME

Currently contracting as a Senior Software Developer for Oppenheimer Funds in Centennial, CO.

I am a front end software developer based out of Denver, CO. Jumping into the Wayback Machine... I started off in early 2000 as a fine artist and abstract painter. I quickly began pursuing a career in Graphic Design focusing on the basics of color theory, layout, typography and more. This built the foundation for what was to become my true passion, UI/UX and Front End Development.



Fast forward to present day, I now have over 10 years of hands-on real world experience in UI/UX design, rapid prototyping and front-end development. I have created websites and applications for a wide range of industries ranging from apparel to health care to the financial industry. I practice user focused design principles basing all my decisions on a strategic blend of user driven need and business requirements. My focus is building responsive mobile first software backed by clean semantic code that always focuses on speed and flexibilty.

In 2005 I opened a small freelance development business in Denver, XIRI Media. Through this company I entertain small and large contracts for businesses around Denver and the US. In the little spare time I have... I develop personal software/app ideas and open source projects.

Aside from all the work stuff, and most importantly in my life... I am a father of two beautiful and healthy girls that keep me on my toes and my life balanced. I love spending every free moment I have with them reading, playing a game on XBOX/PS4, watching some Doctor Who, or just chilling out and drawing or painting. I have also been teaching them HTML/SASS and they are learning it at about 10x the speed I learned... ahhh... to be young again.
